# Mobile QA Checklist

## 375px - iPhone SE

- Hero headline wraps without clipping or overlap
- Reservation dialog fits viewport height and remains scrollable
- Primary and secondary CTAs remain at least 44px tall
- Form inputs stay at or above 16px font size to prevent iOS zoom
- No horizontal scroll on hero, product cards, or footer

## 390px - iPhone 15

- Username availability state remains visible without layout jump
- OTP resend row wraps cleanly without button overflow
- Cookie banner does not cover primary CTA or dialog actions
- Sticky browser chrome does not hide bottom actions

## 430px - iPhone 15 Plus

- Wallet and DEX cards stack cleanly with consistent spacing
- Modal close button stays tappable and visible
- Success state redirect CTA remains fully visible above fold
- Skeleton shimmer and retry buttons render without overflow

## 768px - iPad

- Header nav, hero content, and product cards reflow into tablet layout
- Dialog width stays readable and centered
- Tap targets remain at least 44px
- Scroll behavior stays smooth with no trapped nested scrolling
